FORMULA 1

Formula One, often abbreviated as F1, is the pinnacle of motorsport racing. It involves highly specialized, open-wheel racing cars, known as "Formula One cars," that are designed for maximum speed and performance. F1 races are held on dedicated racetracks or city streets in various countries around the world, making it a global phenomenon. The sport features top-tier racing teams and drivers, each vying for the World Drivers' and Constructors' Championships. Formula One combines cutting-edge technology, strategy, and skill, making it one of the most prestigious and thrilling motorsports in the world.
